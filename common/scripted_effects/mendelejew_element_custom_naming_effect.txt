### He would be dissapointed to learn soon after that another chemist had gotten to the name before him, and as such he fell back on his second choice, Germanium. 
### The "Neptunium" that won the name competition later turned out to be a mere alloy, but by then the name Germanium had already been established. 
### In 1940, a pair of American scientists would create Element 93 in a lab and name it Neptunium, but as that is beyond VictoriaIII's timeframe 
### I'm not too fussed about overriding it. The fact that full realism would demand Neptunium be granted the suffix Np, but I couldn't do that without
### throwing away the similairly historically rich Nipponium, did give me some amount of pause, but ultimately further research proved that element suffix's 
### were more then flexible enough during the period to justify my slight adjustment.
